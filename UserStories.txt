A virtual fridge that keeps track of the food they have in their physical fridge by entering each entry. 
Each entry includes an expiration date if available
Each entry keeps track of who owns the food. 
A shopping list with items that move to the fridge when crossed off. 
Option to color code either based on type of items or owner of items. 
The fridge is synced between every user in the household
The app cannot be updated when offline. 
The app can be viewed offline
